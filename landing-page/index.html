How It Works:

  1. Scout Phase: Scans 700+ markets every 30 minutes, filters by category and liquidity
  2. Strategist Phase: Deep AI analysis on top candidates, estimates fair value and edge
  3. Risk Manager Phase: Kelly Criterion sizing, validates against balance and limits
  4. Executor Phase: Places orders (paper or live), records fees and slippage
  5. Judge Phase: Re-analyzes open positions every 3 minutes, makes exit decisions
